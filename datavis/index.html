Trends in Types of Complaints Regarding Federal Student Loans


Kyle Russo

Over the last four years, there have been 12,479 complaints filed against Navient Solutions, LLC regarding federal student loans, according to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. Many of these might be considered within the context of the Navient lawsuit situation, which included claims that the company failed to apply loan payments to user accounts correctly, among other things. Of these four-years-worth of complaints, most people who made them were in a consensus â€” the primary issue with their federal student loans was that they received bad information about them. Around 23% of complaints regarded this issue from January 2017 to November 2021.

And those issues have remained stagnant over the years, even as overall complaints pertaining to federal student loans drop. In 2017, 22% of complaints regarded this issue. So far in 2021, 24% deal with this issue.

On the other hand, complaints pertaining to the mishandling of payments have seen a dropping trend in more recent years. Such complaints have dropped 6 percentage points since 2018.
